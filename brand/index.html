02 — Logo
02 — 標誌系統

A mark made of yao lines. Yin and yang — also 0 and 1.

以爻為標。陰陽，也是 0 與 1。

The mark is a sign built from the yao lines of the I Ching — the Li trigram (fire / light) means clarity and awareness, exactly what "Rikai" is. The solid line is human judgment, the broken line is AI iteration, stacked into one trigram; it is also binary (code) and "yi" — change, fluency. The top line is vermilion and blinks — at once a moving line and a terminal cursor. Purely geometric, still legible down to 16px, and true to our belief: the code is the proof.

標誌是一枚以易經「爻」構成的記號——離卦（火／明）＝清晰、覺照，正合「理解」。陽爻是人的判斷，陰爻是 AI 的迭代，疊成一卦；同時也是二進位（程式碼）與「易＝變化、通達」。頂爻為朱色且會眨——既是動爻，也是終端機游標；純幾何、縮到 16px 仍清楚，也呼應我們的信念：程式碼即證明。

05 — Naming System
05 — 命名系統

Every name is an act of human perception.

每個名字，都是一個人類知覺的動作。

RikaiDev's strongest identity asset is its naming. Each product takes a native Japanese word, carries layered poetry through its kanji, and lets the word's literal sense map directly to what the tool does. In an age where agents are everywhere and models get swapped out, the name always describes a human act — reading, seeing, watching over — not the technology of the moment. Technology ages; understanding does not.

RikaiDev 最強的識別資產，是它的命名法。每個產品都取一個日文和語、以漢字承載多層詩意，並讓「詞的本義」直接對應工具在做的事。在 agent 遍地、模型會被換掉的時代，名字描述的永遠是人的動作——讀、看、守望——而非當下的技術。技術會過時，理解不會。

一
Name in native Japanese

Take a real Japanese word; its lowercase romaji becomes the product mark.

一
和語為名

取一個真實的日文詞，以小寫羅馬字作為產品標記。

二
Kanji as body

Every name carries kanji, often with layered meanings (読み ＝ 讀／詠／黄泉).

二
漢字為體

名字皆有漢字，且往往一字多義（読み＝讀／詠／黄泉）。

三
Image is function

The word's literal sense maps directly to what the tool does (目線 → point of view).

三
意象即功能

詞的本義直接對應工具在做的事（目線→視角）。

四
The human is the subject

Names describe human perception and care; AI executes, the name is human.

四
以人為主詞

名字描述人的知覺與關懷；AI 是執行，名字是人。

五
Restrained letterforms

Marks are always lowercase sans-serif; kanji appear only in the seal and emblem.

五
克制字形

標記一律小寫無襯線；漢字只在印章與 emblem 出現。

How to name a new product
命名配方
1 → Which single act of perception or care does this tool perform for a person? (read, see, watch over, harvest…)
2 → Find the Japanese word that carries that act; take its kanji.
3 → Make sure the kanji holds a second layer of imagery (the more poetic, the better).
4 → Fix a lowercase romaji as the product mark; the kanji goes into the vermilion seal.
✓ The name speaks of the person; the tool only makes that act real.
1 → 這個工具幫人做的，是哪一個「知覺或關懷的動作」？（讀、看、守望、收成…）
2 → 找出承載那個動作的日文和語，取其漢字。
3 → 確認漢字有第二層意象可延伸（越詩意越好）。
4 → 以小寫羅馬字定為產品標記，漢字入朱印。
✓ 名字說的是人；工具，只是讓那個動作成真。
06 — Graphic Language
06 — 圖形語言

Thin lines, a square seal, and the breathing room of one stroke.

細線、方印、與一筆的餘白。

Line icons
線圖標

1.5px even-weight ink strokes, 2px rounded corners, square terminals. Monochrome, unfilled, no shadow.

1.5px 等粗細墨線、圓角 2px、方形收邊。單色，不填色，不加陰影。

Square-seal motif
方印母題

The vermilion square seal works as a repeating motif, with slight random rotation (±3°) for a handmade imperfection.

朱色方印可作為重複母題，微幅隨機旋轉（±3°）帶來手作的不完美。

Hairline grid
細線網格

Divide the layout with hairline rules instead of filled cards. Whitespace leads, lines support.

以髮絲級細線分割版面，取代填色卡片。留白為主，線為輔。
